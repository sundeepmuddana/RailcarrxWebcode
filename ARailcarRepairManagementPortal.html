Union Tank Car Company Partners with RailcarRx to Build Repair Link – A Railcar Repair Management Portal

DALLAS, TX: Union Tank Car Company (UTLX), North America’s leading tank car manufacturer and lessor launches UTLX Repair Link-a BRC and railcar management portal to streamline its transactions with contract shops during the repair cycle. Technology driven solutions are often born from a need to replace or reduce the dependency on existing processes that are no longer scalable and efficient. Before Repair Link, the repair cycle of a car involved several communication steps between UTLX and the shops across multiple platforms including email and phone to exchange repair instructions, estimates and invoices. With a fleet of over 80,000 cars they needed a robust and efficient system.

UTLX turned to RailcarRx, a leading technology solution provider in the rail industry to streamline process, enhance automation and increase profitability. UTLX was already using RailcarRx’s Repair Shop Management System for repair rebilling and relied on their expertise in technology and knowledge of the rail industry to design a shop portal. The project was planned in multiple phases which are already live. Several shops are currently onboard with the system and more are being added.

“We have been using RailcarRx’s Repair Shop Solution to help us consolidate rebill practices. It was a logical step to see how we might also leverage the expertise of the RailcarRx team to help manage the repair process for shops outside of our own network. This new system allows us to tightly manage the repair process and provide UTLX with more auditing capabilities,” says Rick Koenig, Director of Fleet Repair and AAR Services at UTLX.

Shops are now able to access railcar information, upload estimates and invoices, and communicate with UTLX-all through one single streamlined platform. UTLX plans to have all their contract repair shops on board with Repair Link within the year.

Josh Lippy, Senior Director of Business Development at RailcarRx says “Partnering with Union Tank Car provides us with the opportunity to showcase our capabilities. We already had framework in place that allowed us to build this new system quickly and offer it to other railcar equipment owners as well.”

About RailcarRx: RailcarRx is a technology solution provider for repair shops, railcar owners and railroads in North America. They specialize in out-of-the box and custom cloud based solutions in the rail industry. Some of the largest players in the industry utilize RailcarRx solutions to optimize revenue and improve efficiency. For more information visit http://www.railcarrx.com.
