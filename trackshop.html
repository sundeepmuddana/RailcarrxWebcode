Track shop performance to make smart decisions

How RailcarRx RMS’ pre-defined reporting features can support every step of your repair management

Managing a repair facility can be really complicated when there is little visibility across the entire shop operation including actionable insights on key performance indicators that are critical to the growth of the business.  It’s hard to keep customers happy if you are still creating reports manually without a system to generate information quickly when they need it.

One of the major functions that RailcarRx Repair Management System provides is a robust reporting system.  RailcarRx RMS has 47 out-of-box reports for every key aspect of the shop operation, such as equipment and workflow management at different shop locations, accounting and invoicing management, and inventory and labor management.  Our goal is for you to easily access the information your customers need and to provide the actionable insights to improve operational efficiencies.

 

Now imagine, with a cup of fresh coffee in the morning, generating the following reports with just one click.

__________________________________

Communicate with customers effectively and quickly

When your customers ask the question where their cars are in the repair process, you can impress them with providing that information and much more than just repair status.  With RailcarRx’s detailed Custom Status Report, you are able to provide your customers with all the details about their cars in the shop location, including the current repair status and the workflow status details from the arrival of the car through inspection, repair, invoicing and disposition. The report also provides other details like days in the shop, estimated hours, repair hours and estimated dollar amount.  Of course, you can obtain all the information above with just one click.

 

Manage inventory efficiently and plan ahead

The entire process of inventory management can be complicated yet so critical to profitability, from ordering, tracking parts, to managing overstock.  That’s why RMS includes more than 10 reporting features to help you better manage inventory.

To start with, the Material Items Report provides a very clear visibility on all of your inventory including quantities, costs and inventory values.  To help you plan and make smart shopping decisions. The Material Below Threshold Report serves as a detailed material shopping list with part item code, name, description and quantity.  This report tells you which parts are low inventory and needed for on-going repairs so enough materials are always available for scheduled repairs.  It enables both purchasing and the repair team to improve work efficiencies.  To help you better manage the overstock, the Material Not Moving Report lists the material items in stock that are “slow movers” in the recent 1,2 or 3 months and their last order, quantity in stock, and last used quantities and other details.

 

Track a variety of key factors that are of concern to management

RMS has more than 10 management reports built in the system to track key performance indicators.  The reports contain valuable information about employee performance, accounting and financial summaries and shop performance at different locations.  For example, the Cost Analysis Report provides profit and loss analysis at the equipment level or customer level so you’ll gain an actionable insight on where you can do better to maximize the profitability.  The report includes details such as billed labor hours, actual hours, labor and material charges, gross revenue and profit.  In addition, shop owners and managers can control users access to any reports based on their roles.

 

How we take the reporting features to the next level

Most reports are customizable with simple filtering options so users can obtain the reports the way they need them.  What’s more,  besides all the powerful reporting features built in the system, we can also provide customized reports and analysis to meet your specific needs.
